關於本系統 About

本系統為國立陽明交通大學腦科學研究所授權教師提供線上評分平台，協助教師記錄並管理所指導學生的評分。

The NYCU IBS Rotation Scoring System is an online platform for faculty members at the Institute of Brain Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU IBS). It enables authorized instructors to record and manage scores for the students they supervise.

評分紀錄Grade Recording

Submit numerical scores and written feedback for each supervised student.

📋

名單管理Roster Management

查看並維護指派給您的學生名單，確保評分資料準確完整。

View and manage the student list assigned to you for organized and accurate record-keeping.
